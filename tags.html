Glossary of tags I'm using and why they matter to me

quantified-self

"Quantified self" refers to self tracking and coming up with metrics in order to gain more insight and potentially improve various aspects of your life such as health (physical or mental) or performance.

I personally haven't managed to gain any signingicant insights from the data I've collected so far. However, I find the very process of trying to quantify motivating for routine such as exercise, sleep or diet, and inspiring for learning more about them.

pkm

PKM stands for Personal Knowledge Management (I agree it's a somewhat clumsy acronym). It refers to strategies and tools for effecient information management, which is particularly important these days in abundance of infomration around us.

Specifically, when I talk about it I mostly refer to digital knowledge management and ways of seamless integration with our meaty brains. I write more about it here.
