mm, food. To be honest, if I had infinite amount of free time, I'd always cooked by myself or with friends. However, that's not the case yet, so I have to use food deliveries too.

So, you fancy some soup, beef and perhaps some green veggies like broccoli. First reflex is to go to deliveroo and search 'beef'. except, there is no search. fucking websites these days. I'm sure that is some marketing directed decision of course intended to engage you into clicking throught various restaurants more. so what do people do? I'd imagine TODO pathetic choose one of 'categories'. What do I do for beef, 'steak'?? It's also shocking to some people, but steak is not the only edible form of beef.

Anyway, then you open all the 15 restaurants that serve steak in different tabs and try to Ctrl-F like some kind of TOOD metaphor.

Fucking hell, не для того моя роза цвела

After spending a while on that I figured it's not even that time saving, after all, cooking steak by yourself takes few minutes. So obviously, I automated that.
